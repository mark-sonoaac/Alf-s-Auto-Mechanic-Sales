# Alf's Auto Mechanic & Sales — Website

This is the official website for **Alf's Auto Mechanic & Sales LLC**, located at 556 Hawthorne Ave, Newark, NJ 07112.

---

## What's Included

The site has the following pages:

- **Home** — Hero section with image slider, services overview, inventory highlight, about, and contact info
- **Services** — Full list of repair services offered
- **Cars for Sale** — Browsable car inventory with filtering by make, model, price, and mileage. Includes a featured slider and financing pre-approval form
- **Book a Repair** — Appointment request form where customers select services, date, and time
- **My Repairs** — Repair tracking page showing job status and cost breakdown
- **Contact** — Full contact info (phone, email, hours, social links) and a message form

---

## Important Note — Frontend Only

This website is currently **frontend only**. That means:

- All pages and content display correctly in the browser
- **Forms do not send data anywhere yet** — when a customer submits a contact form, books a repair, or submits an inquiry, it shows a confirmation message on screen but no email or notification is sent
- The car inventory and repair records are built into the site for display purposes
- A backend (server + database) will need to be connected in a future phase to make forms fully functional, store appointments, and manage live inventory
